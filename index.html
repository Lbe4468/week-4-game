Crystals Collector Game

HOW TO PLAY

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. Clicking on one of the four crystals below will add a specific amount of points to your score.
  3. You win by matching your score to the random number. You lose if your score goes above the random number.
  4. the value of each crystal is unknown until the crystal is clicked. Upon the start of each new game the random number as well as the crystal values are changed.
